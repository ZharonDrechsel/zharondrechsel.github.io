Background

Not a computer scientist.
Something different.

My foundation is anthropology — the study of human communication systems, behavior, and meaning-making. That lens shapes everything I build.

Where most software starts with data structures, I start with the question: how do people actually communicate, and what does a system need to understand to meet them there?

The result is software that feels less like a tool and more like a conversation.
